我有一只小狗。
小狗叫多多。
多多很小。
多多很白。
我说："多多！"
多多来了！
我说："喝水！"
多多喝水了。
多多是我的好朋友。
我很爱多多！

I have a little dog.
The dog is called Duo Duo.
Duo Duo is very small.
Duo Duo is very white.
I said, "Duo Duo!"
Duo Duo came!
I said, "Drink some water!"
Duo Duo drank water.
Duo Duo is my best friend.
I love Duo Duo very much!
